**Title:** AI-MIME — GIF Reactions for AI Chats

**Short description (132 chars):**
Adds fun GIF reactions to your AI chats. Works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok & DeepSeek. Your AI gets a personality!

**Full description:**
AI-MIME watches your AI conversations and reacts with perfectly-matched GIFs.

When ChatGPT explains a code bug, you see "this is fine" dog. When Claude apologizes, a sad violin plays. When Gemini gets excited about your idea — mic drop.

How it works:
1. Install the extension — no other downloads needed
2. Chat with any supported AI
3. Watch as AI-MIME reacts with 2-3 contextual GIFs in a floating overlay

15 free AI-powered reactions per day. Add a free OpenRouter key for unlimited.

Supported sites:
- ChatGPT (chatgpt.com)
- Claude (claude.ai)
- Google Gemini (gemini.google.com)
- Grok (grok.com)
- DeepSeek (chat.deepseek.com)

Privacy-first: Only reads AI responses (never your messages). No accounts, no tracking, no analytics.

Open source: https://github.com/Deefunxion/ai-mime-v2
